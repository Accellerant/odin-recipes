How to make a Hawaiian Pizza - Recipe

Have you ever wanted to make a fool proof hawaiian pizza but didn't know how to make one?

This recipe will demystify the mysticism of creating a bodacious pie with both sweet and savory flavors!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of pizza dough
  • Olive Oil for Brushing and Greasing Tray
  • Cornmeal - Sprinkle Baking Sheet (optional)
  • 1/2 cup pizza or marinara sauce
  • 1 Cup Mozzarella Cheese - Shredded
  • 4 Ounces Canadian Bacon - 4-5 slices
  • 1/2 cup diced pineapples, or 100g
  • 1/4 Cup Chopped Bacon - About 4 Slices
  • Oregano - Fresh or Sliced for Sprinkling

Steps

  1. Preheat Oven to 500°F/260°C
  2. Set Rack to Lower Position
  3. Let Dough Get to Room Temeprature: 30 mins
  4. Lightly Brush Baking Sheet with oil
  5. Sprinkle Cornmeal onto pan if desired
  6. Lightly Flour a Surface, shape dough to 10 in circle
  7. Transer Dough to Baking Sheet, press dough to 12 in
  8. Brush oil on edges of dough
  9. Spread Sauce atop dough
  10. Place cheese, ham, pineapple and bacon atop sauce
  11. If desired, sprinkle oregano
  12. Place Pizza into oven
  13. Cook 'till golden brown, about 10-12 minutes
  14. Take out and let cool for five minutes
  15. Serve!
