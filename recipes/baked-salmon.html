Baked Salmon

How to make Oven Baked Salmon

Sometimes, you want to make a complicated meal.

Othertimes, ya just want something simple as fuck.

This meal's the latter baby!

Ingredients

  • 12 oz fillet, cut into 4 pieces
  • Coarse-Grained Salt
  • Freshly Ground Black Pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees Farenheit
  2. Season salmon with salt and pepper.
  3. Place salmon ski nside down onto non-stick pan
  4. Bake until thoroughly cooked though, 12-15 minutes
