Mashed Potatoes

How to Make Mashed Potatoes

Do normal carbs make you sad 'cause they aren't mushy?

This recipe's got ya covered for tasty mash!

Ingredients

  • 5 Pounds of Potatoes
  • 2 Large Cloves Garlic - Minced
  • Fine Sea Salt
  • 6 Tablespoons of Butter
  • 1 Cup of Whole Milk
  • 4 oz of cream cheese - room temperature
  • Toppings - Chives, green onions, black pepper

Instructions

  1. Cut those potats into even sized chunks!
  2. Transfer to a large stock pot of cold water
  3. Shove the Garlic and Salt into the water
  4. Heat 'till their sins begin to make 'em boil
  5. Reduce heat to Medium-high, cook for 10-12 minutes
  6. Potats should give way easy to a knoife
  7. Drain out all the sin water
  8. Heat the milk, butter, and 2 TSP of salt
  9. Once just melted, set aside
  10. Return Potatos to the stockpot
  11. Set the heat to low for 1 minute, then remove
  12. Mash the FUCK out of the potats 'till smooth!
  13. Pour half of your melted butter shit onto potatos
  14. Mix together 'till potats soaked up the butter
  15. Add the rest of that nasty butter water
  16. Do the same thing with the cream cheese!
  17. Taste that fine ass load of carbs
  18. Become enlightened
  19. Add more of the seasonings if needed
  20. Serve as is, add topings if ya want foo!
